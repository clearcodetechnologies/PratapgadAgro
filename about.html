Welcome to Pratapgad Agro

Pratapgad Agro Pvt Ltd. is Agro Retail Company. Under the umbrella of services, company provides agronomic services including soil testing, crop inspection, weather forecasts and advisory services on farm credit, crop loan, life insurance and medical Insurance etc. Pratapgad Agro is pioneered in providing quality fresh produce to the market through its vast network and has diversified itself in the line of Contract farming for Horticulture products as well as wheat and rice. Serves farmers by facilitating their all agricultural needs and provides free agricultural consultancy services ranging from sowing to harvesting of crops.

Vision

Pratapgad Agro's Vision is to be a trusted agriculture company approach in agriculture input , services, product distribution , consultancy and agriculture solutions provider by educating farmers on minimizing their cost of cultivation without compromising on quality & yield through maximum utilization of available farming resources.

Mission

Pratapgad Agro's Mission is to increase the productivity of farmland & farmers by providing individual guidance to the farmers for individual farming, to be one of the India’s leading fresh fruits and vegetables growing company. To provide quality agri-inputs and timely services to farmers. To improve the lifestyle & economic scalability of farmers. To make those farmers prosper, who actually does farming.

We Care

Pratapgad Agro cares for Medical insurance of farmers, facilitating their all agricultural needs and provides free agricultural consultancy services ranging from sowing to harvesting of crops.
